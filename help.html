Quick Start

Type any city, town, or landmark into the forecast search box and press enter to see the latest conditions and 5-day outlook.

  • Use full place names for the most accurate results.
  • Tap a forecast card to refresh the data while it is open.
  • Switch between light and dark themes via the toggle in the header.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign in?

Visit the login page and enter the shared beta credentials listed in the product brief. Successful sign-ins are stored locally in your browser as JSON so you stay signed in during this session.

Where is my data stored?

Login sessions are kept in your browser storage only. Forecast searches are sent directly from your device to the Open-Meteo API and are never logged by SkyCast.

Why am I not seeing results for some towns?

Smaller towns may not be included in the global database yet. Try the nearest major city or use the name of a nearby landmark.

Can I report an issue?

Absolutely. Send an email to feedback@skycast.app with the location you searched and what you expected to see.
