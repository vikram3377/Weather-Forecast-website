Our Mission

SkyCast delivers precise, hyper-local forecasts in a clean interface that respects your attention and adapts to any device.

We combine modern browser capabilities with community-backed weather data to surface the details that matter: current conditions, reliable trends, and quick comparisons at a glance.

How It Works

SkyCast uses the Open-Meteo API suite for both global geocoding and 7-day weather forecasts. We fetch and render data directly in your browser‚Äîno servers or third-party trackers in between.

  • Geocoding: Searches are resolved to precise coordinates via the Open-Meteo geocoding API.
  • Forecasts: Live current conditions and multi-day forecasts are pulled on demand and cached in memory.
  • Privacy-first: Your queries never leave your device beyond the direct API call to Open-Meteo.

Technology Stack

Built without frameworks, SkyCast relies on platform-native tools for performance and reliability.

  • HTML for semantic structure and accessibility.
  • Modern CSS with responsive layout, fluid typography, and dual light/dark themes.
  • Vanilla JavaScript to orchestrate data fetching, state management, and user interactions.
